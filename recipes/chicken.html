Bill's Chicken Marinade

Description

A perfect marinade for making delicious barbeque chicken. Perfected by Bill Graham, aka 'Papa'.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup light soy sauce
  • 1 Tpsp chili powder
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/4 tsp liquid smoke
  • 1/4 tsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. Add olive oil, light soy sauce, liquid smoke and sesame oil to a container large enough to hold your chicken.
  2. Peel garlic cloves and press with a garlic press. If you don't have access to a garlic press you can finely chop the cloves instead. Add pressed/chopped garlic to your other ingredients.
  3. Add your chicken to the marinade and coat thouroughly.
  4. Let chicken marinate in refrigerator for at least two hours, but preferable overnight. Flip chicken over halfway through to be sure both sides are covered evenly.
  5. Cook chicken as you would normally, prefereably by barbequeing it.
  6. Enjoy!
