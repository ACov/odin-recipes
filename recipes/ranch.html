Ranch Dressing

Description

Spicy buttermilk ranch dressing. Creamy dressing with a bit of a kick. Great for salads, veggies and wings.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp Hidden Valley Buttermilk Ranch Dressing Mix
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/8 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1/8 tsp paprika

Steps

  1. Put mayonnaise and buttermilk into a medium sized bowl and stir with a fork or whisk to combine.
  2. Add contents of ranch packet, black pepper, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, and paprika. Stir or wisk until ingredients are well mixed.
  3. Refrigerate for 30 minutes to allow dressing to thicken.
  4. Dressing will keep in the fridge for up to two weeks.
